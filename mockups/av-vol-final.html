Final — Volume Drawer (iPhone) + Side Rail (TSW)

Same speaker-icon + % in the header on both form factors. Tap it → iPhone drops down a drawer, TSW slides out a side rail from the right. Both contain the same controls: a readonly slider for volume feedback, a mute toggle, and dedicated Vol Up / Vol Down buttons that pulse the digital joins (analog goes one direction SIMPL → panel only). If SIMPL doesn't send feedback, the slider can be hidden and the drawer / rail will show just mute + up / down.
